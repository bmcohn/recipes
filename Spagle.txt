Grilled Sesame Soy Sauce Aspergrass

Ingerdients
-Aspergrass
-Seasame Seeds
-Soy Sauce

Instructions
Cut ends off of Aspergrass
Place aspergrass on tin foil
Springle on toasted seasame seeds
Drizzle on a little Soy sauce
Cover with tin foil folding into container
Place on hot gill for 5 min each side or until cooked
